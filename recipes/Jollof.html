Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish featuring rice cooked in a spiced tomato sauce. Its rich, savory flavor comes from a blend of tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers, often enhanced with curry and thyme.

This vibrant dish is typically enjoyed with chicken, beef, or vegetables and is a favorite at gatherings and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 pound tomatoes, blended
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 4 cups chicken stock or water
  • 1-2 bouillon cubes or 1 tablespoon bouillon powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1/2 pound chicken, beef, or vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and saut√© onions, bell pepper, and garlic until soft.
  3. Add blended tomatoes, tomato paste, curry powder, thyme, and bay leaves. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Add chicken stock, bouillon cubes, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Stir in the rice, cover, and reduce heat to low. Cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the sauce.
  6. If using, add cooked chicken, beef, or vegetables and mix well.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve hot and enjoy!
